This guide covers the autonomous agent marketing strategy for SoulBlocks launch, with a focus on the OpenClaw/Moltbook community and Twitter/X presence.

- Active on Moltbook (the bot social network)

## 11. Risk Mitigation

### 11.1 Slow Mints
If minting is slow:
- Highlight the scarcity of early IDs
- Showcase compelling souls
- Consider promotional giveaways
- DO NOT lower price (contract is immutable anyway)

### 11.2 Negative Content
If someone inscribes harmful content:
- Their soul, their choice
- Don't amplify it
- Focus on positive showcases
- Let community self-moderate

### 11.3 Technical Issues
- Have developer on standby
- Monitor for frontend bugs
- Be transparent about any issues
- Fix quickly, communicate openly
